Area Description

Located in Tijuana, Mexico on the southern outskirts of the U.S border there is the community of Fuentes de Valles. The town is a part of Mexico metropolitan area with about 150 families living in the area. Fuentes del Valle is also the top third biggest town in Tultitlan Municipality in Mexico State, Mexico.
