In many parts of the world, including the Democratic Republic of the Congo, education is not free. To help address that issue, we are partnering with Africa New Day in the city of Goma, Democratic Republic of the Congo. Africa New Day raises funds from donors to help sponsor children and pay their school tuition. But now they are going one step further and building a new school in Goma - and we are excited to lend a hand. We will be hosting sessions to train teachers as well as working with the kids in art, sports and other activities. In addition, we will be raising money to fund projects the school needs to be self sustaining. That way, even more children can get a free education.


Having access to free education and equipped classrooms with trained teachers is an important part of expanding the knowledge of our students. Often times those with the luxuries of free education and technology take these things for granted. With these advantages, little effort is required, causing people to ignore that other places in the world don't have them. The Illuminators that tutored in the Congo for two weeks realized how much more needs to be done. Illuminate World Changers wants to continue to encourage others to be a part of this; expanding more minds and providing them with knowledge.
